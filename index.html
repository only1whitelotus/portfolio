About Me

Hey there, I'm Akinola Akinjide, also referred to as White Lotus. I like to refer to myself as a digital creator because I bring imaginations and ideas to life through designs, illustrations, motion graphics, and videos. A simple title such as graphics designer or creative director doesn't do justice in defining my role in the digital world, so I simply say I'm a creative with limitless imagination and endless possibilities.

Design Values

Storytelling and User Centered Designs: I love creating designs that tell a story and resonate with people. It's all about making sure the audience connects with what they're seeing in a meaningful way.
Continuous Learning: I'm very keen on always learning and growing in the field of design. I do this by practicing new design trends, exploring other design fields, and by reading about emerging technology
Innovation and Simplicity: I’m all about blending creativity with simplicity. I aim to make designs that are eye-catching, unique, but also easy to understand at a glance.
Consistency: I make sure everything looks like it belongs together, whether it's a logo, social media post, or animation. Keeping things consistent helps build strong, recognizable brands.
